⚠️ When Uber & Lyft Prices Spike — ES Rates Stay the Same

Uber and Lyft use dynamic pricing to balance supply and demand in real time. When demand exceeds available drivers — during rush hour, bad weather, or major events — fares rise automatically. Uber calls this “Surge Pricing.” Lyft calls it “Prime Time.”

ES Transportation charges the same flat rate no matter when or where you ride. Common situations where dynamic pricing drives fares up:

🌙 Late Night / Early Morning 🎉 Holidays & Weekends 🌧️ Bad Weather 🎵 Concerts & Events ✈️ Peak Travel Days 🏈 Game Days
