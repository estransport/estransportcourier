💳 Simple, Transparent & No Surprises

We believe you should know exactly what you're paying — before, during, and after your delivery. No hidden fees, no last-minute charges, no confusion. Here's how it works:

💳 Payment: PayPal (Credit/Debit Cards) or Zelle only. No cash. $20.00 booking fee required before service begins, applied toward your total.
⏱️ Service Timing: Rush — pickup within 2 hrs after booking fee paid. Standard — within 4 hrs. Next Day — before 12:00 PM.
