Pricing (Point A → Point B)

Pricing is based on the one-way distance between pickup (Point A) and delivery (Point B).

  • Base Rate (Up to 10 miles): $30
  • 11–25 miles: $45
  • 26–35 miles: $60
  • 36–50 miles: $75
  • 51–75 miles: $125

Rush Service: +$10

Booking Fee: $20 (applied toward total)

Maximum Distance: 75 miles. Deliveries beyond 75 miles require a custom quote.

Service Areas

Primary: Houston, Cypress, Katy, Sugar Land

Extended Service Areas: The Woodlands, Kingwood, Spring, Klein, Tomball, Humble, Aldine, Richmond

Extended Area Fee: An additional $20 fee applies when pickup or delivery is in an Extended Service Area. This fee is in addition to the calculated distance-based pricing between Point A and Point B.

Example: Pickup in Katy (Primary) to delivery in The Woodlands (Extended) = Distance-based rate + $20 Extended Area Fee

How Booking Works

Step 1: Submit your pickup request using the form below

Step 2: Receive quote & booking confirmation via email (typically within 15-30 minutes)

Step 3: Pay $20 booking fee via PayPal or Zelle (payment instructions included in email)

Step 4: Pickup is scheduled based on your selected service type:

  • Rush: Pickup within 2 hours after booking fee is paid
  • Standard: Pickup within 4 hours after booking fee is paid
  • Next Day: Pickup anytime from booking until 12:00 PM the next day

Payment & Cancellation Policy

Booking Fee: $20.00 (applied toward total fare)

Payment Methods: PayPal or Zelle

Payment Timing: Paid after receiving quote and booking confirmation email with payment instructions

Cancellation Policy:

  • Rush & Standard Services: Full refund of booking fee if cancelled within 1 hour of payment. No refund for cancellations after 1 hour from payment or no-shows.
  • Next Day Service: Full refund of booking fee if cancelled before 8:00 PM on the day before pickup. No refund for cancellations after 8:00 PM or no-shows.

Item Requirements & Restrictions

Important: All items must be specified and approved at the time of booking. If the items presented at pickup do not match what was agreed upon during booking, the service will be cancelled and the booking fee will not be refunded.

Delivery Confirmation Options

  • Photo Proof of Delivery: Available for all deliveries
  • Hand-Off Confirmation: Available based on delivery location
  • Signature Required: Available upon request

Prohibited Items

  • Illegal substances
  • Firearms or ammunition
  • Hazardous or explosive materials
  • Live animals

Fragile Items: Handled with care upon request. Please specify fragile items in your pickup request.
