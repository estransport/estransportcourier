Extra Stop (Add-On Service) Policy

  • An Extra Stop is an optional add-on service separate from the primary pickup and final delivery locations and is not included as part of the core pickup and final delivery service once attempted or cancelled in route.
  • The $10.00 Extra Stop fee covers one delivery attempt during active dispatch.
  • If the courier arrives at the Extra Stop location and no authorized party is available, the Extra Stop is considered a completed service attempt. The courier will proceed to the final delivery location unless otherwise instructed.
  • If the Extra Stop is cancelled while the courier is en route, the Extra Stop portion of the service is considered cancelled in route and the courier will proceed to the final delivery location.
  • If the customer requests a return visit to the Extra Stop location after a failed or cancelled attempt, the return visit is treated as a new service attempt and a separate $10.00 redelivery fee will apply.
  • Any outstanding balance must be paid in full, along with the applicable $10.00 redelivery fee, prior to scheduling and dispatching the return trip.
  • Additional redelivery attempts may require a new booking.
