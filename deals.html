Terms & Conditions

First Delivery Discount: $10.00 off the remaining balance for first-time customers only. One per customer. $20.00 booking fee still required. Cannot be combined with other offers.

Referral Credit: Both the referring customer and the new customer receive $5.00 off their next respective remaining balance. New customer must complete their first delivery for the referring customer’s credit to activate. Cannot be combined with other offers.
