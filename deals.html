Terms & Conditions

First Delivery Discount (Courier): $10.00 off the remaining balance for first-time courier customers only. One per customer. $20.00 booking fee still required. Cannot be combined with other offers.

Driver Welcome Offer: New passengers only. Applied automatically on the first 2 non-airport rides. On Flavor Guide or City Trip rides, the applicable promo code (FLAVOR15 or CITYTRIPS15) stacks with the welcome bonus for maximum savings. On all other non-airport rides, $10.00 off the remaining balance per ride, issued as a post-trip credit. Non-transferable. Cannot be combined with AIRPORT10.

FLAVOR15: 15% off the trip fare (remaining balance only, excluding the $20 booking fee) on Flavor Guide rides. Valid on every qualifying booking — ongoing, no expiry. Not valid on airport transfers. One code per booking.

CITYTRIPS15: 15% off the trip fare (remaining balance only, excluding the $20 booking fee) on City Trip rides. Valid on every qualifying booking — ongoing, no expiry. Not valid on airport transfers. One code per booking.

AIRPORT10: $10.00 off the remaining balance on each of the first 2 airport trips. New passengers only. Applied as a post-trip refund. Does not stack with the non-airport welcome offer. Valid for IAH and Hobby (HOU) transfers only.

Referral Credit: Both the referring customer and the new customer receive $5.00 off their next respective remaining balance. Applies to courier deliveries and driver orders. New customer must complete their first order for the referring customer’s credit to activate. Cannot be combined with other offers.

Loyalty Reward: $10.00 off the remaining balance on the 6th order, then resets. Courier deliveries and driver orders both count toward the 5-order threshold. Orders must be fully completed and paid to count. Credit is non-transferable.

General: Only one discount per order unless explicitly noted as stackable above. All discounts apply to the remaining balance, not the $20.00 booking fee. ES Transportation reserves the right to modify or discontinue offers at any time.
